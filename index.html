How to Use PawSense

Two ways to monitor your dog's emotions

📸 Option 1 — Upload a Photo
1. Click the Upload Photo tab
2. Tap the upload box and choose a photo of your dog
3. Click Analyse Emotion
4. See the result on the right!
📡 Option 2 — Live iPhone Camera
1. Open this app on your iPhone in Safari
2. Click the Live Camera tab
3. Choose how often to analyse (every 5–30 seconds)
4. Click Start Monitoring
5. Point your iPhone at your dog — it will automatically detect their emotion and update the results!
6. Click Stop when done
💡 Tip — Best results
• Make sure your dog's full body is visible
• Use good lighting
• Keep the camera steady
• The app works best when your dog is doing something natural, not just sitting still
